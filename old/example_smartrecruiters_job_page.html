Job Description

Proposed Work Location: Census Bureau Headquarters, Suitland, Maryland

SNAP is pursuing a contract with the U.S. Census Bureau to provide an experienced Sr. Business and Solution Architect . The preferred candidate will possess prior Census Bureau experience, excellent
people and leadership skills, and great communication and presentation skills. This position will lead and will serve as a mentor for a team of Business and Solution Architects on a large program supported by more than 100 full-time employees.

The selected candidate will perform the following duties (this is not a comprehensive list of all duties to be performed):

  • Assist in the documentation and establishment of processes, templates and tools required to develop and manage business and solution architecture.

  • Ensure architecture supporting major programs in the Decennial Directorate are in alignment and compliance with the Bureau’s enterprise architecture.

  • Ensure architecture supporting major programs in the Decennial Directorate are in alignment and compliance with the programs’ business and operations plans, schedules, documentation, and governance.

  • Support the development of business and solution architecture designs.

  • Provide expertise in business process and analytical modeling concepts, techniques and tools.

  • Develop and maintain business process models, interface data flow documentation (e.g. data exchanges and system interconnections), Integrated Operations diagrams and functional and technical diagrams.
